Sponsorship Opportunities

Great Hearts offers sponsorship opportunities for corporations, foundations and individuals. Sponsorship provides a way to meet your firm’s philanthropic and marketing goals. By sponsoring Great Hearts, you are helping to expand the giving community.

Founding Sponsorship

Founding sponsor contributions are used to support our core initiatives. Your contribution enables 100 percent of the individual’s gift to go directly to the cause or nonprofit. This type of sponsorship gives prominent recognition to your firm but is not tax deductible since Great Hearts is a social enterprise rather than a nonprofit organization.

Sponsorship of the Givers’ Choice Award

Sponsorship of a Giver’s Choice Award is fully tax deductible. 100 percent of the contribution goes to the nonprofit organizations that are selected for the award. As a sponsor, you may either select a specific category of causes (e.g. health, homelessness, conservation) or provide funding for a general (all causes are eligible) award.

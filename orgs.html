Nonprofit Organizations

Thanks for considering Great Hearts Initiatives!

Becoming part of the Great Hearts community is an important way to support the efforts of your nonprofit organization. We do that by bringing together people and causes into an inclusive, online community. We believe that everyone has something give and have developed many options for doing so.

Once your organization is registered, you will be able to promote your cause, highlight specific needs, request contributions, and engage volunteers.

In September 2016, the Great Hearts community will launch with a founding group of members and organizations. You’ll be part of it. Your organizations will have the opportunity to:

  • Complete a profile page on which you may highlight your mission, goals, key initiatives, and needs. Doing so will help promote your cause and attract Great Hearts community members’ engagement.
  • Participate in the Givers’ Choice Award. This innovative monetary award is member-led. It provides an opportunity for all nonprofits to be considered based on Great Hearts community members’ recommendations.
  • Receive financial contributions from Great Hearts community members. All financial contributions that are generated via Great Hearts go directly to your organization. As a 100 percent giving platform, no set up fees or administrative costs are taken out by Great Hearts. Additionally, community members will be encouraged to contribute directly to your organization so that credit card processing fees aren’t incurred.

During July and August, watch for updates. Also, with your consent, we’ll showcase your organization with Great Hearts members. Thanks for all the good work that you are doing to make the world the better place. Great Hearts is honored to celebrate and support you.


Barb Lorsbach
Co-Founder and CEO, Great Hearts Initiatives

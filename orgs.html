Be a Part Great Hearts Community and Givers’ Network!

Becoming part of the Great Hearts community is an excellent way to support and promote your nonprofit organization. In September 2016, the Great Hearts community and givers’ network will launch with a founding group of people and organizations. We believe that everyone has something give and have developed many options for doing so.
All US-based 501(C)3 nonprofits that are in good standing are eligible to be part of the Great Hearts community. There are no fees for participating. Once registered, your organizations will have the opportunity to:
  • Promote your cause by completing a Great Hearts web page on which you may highlight your mission, goals, key initiatives, and needs. Doing so will attract community members’ to learn about you, recommend your organization, and to get involved with your activities.
  • Participate in the Givers’ Choice Award. For this innovative monetary award, Great Hearts community members select the winning nonprofits. A new award is given monthly and more often based on sponsor contributions.
  • Receive financial contributions from Great Hearts community members. Although Great Hearts is not a crowd funding site, it does provide a good way for community members to financial give to your organization. As a 100 percent giving platform, all financial contributions that are generated via Great Hearts go directly to your nonprofit. No set up fees or administrative costs are taken out. Additionally, community members will be encouraged to contribute directly to your organization so that credit card processing fees aren’t incurred.
During July and August, watch for updates. Also, with your consent, your nonprofit will be showcased so that Great Hearts community members start to get to know about your organization.
Thanks for all the good work that you are doing. Let us know how Great Hearts can be helpful.

Barb Lorsbach
Co-Founder and CEO, Great Hearts Initiatives

Nonprofit Organizations

Be a Part of Great Hearts Community and Giver's Network!

Becoming part of the Great Hearts community is way to support and promote your nonprofit organization. In September 2016, the Great Hearts community and giver's network will launch with a founding group of people and organizations. We believe that everyone has something to give and have developed many options for doing so.
All US-based 501(C)3 nonprofits that are in good standing are eligible to be part of the Great Hearts community. There are no fees for participating.

Benefits of Being a Part of Great Hearts

Once registered, your organizations will have the opportunity to:
  • Promote your cause by completing a Great Hearts web page on which you may highlight your mission, goals, key initiatives, and needs. Doing so will attract Great Hearts community members to learn about your organization, to get involved with your activities, and provide other forms of support.
  • Participate in the Giver's Choice Award. For this innovative monetary award, Great Hearts community members select the winning nonprofit. Awards are given throughout the year. The first award pool will be for $25,000 and will be given in November 2016. Organizations participating in our beta launch will be eligible for the first award. We are now accepting a limited number of organizations for the beta launch.
  • Receive financial contributions from Great Hearts community members. Although Great Hearts is not a crowdfunding site, it does provide a good way for community members to financially give to your organization. All financial contributions that are generated via Great Hearts go directly to your nonprofit. No set up fees or administrative costs are taken out.

What Great Hearts Asks of Your Organization

We ask that your organization actively participate in Great Hearts community activities by:
  • Completing your organizational profile.
  • Frequently update your profile with your programs, needs and events.
  • Help foster the Great Hearts community by sharing it with your volunteers, board members, staff and others who will support you for the Giver's Choice Award.
  • Be supportive of our community guidelines so that Great Hearts can be a helpful, safe and inclusive place for inspiration, giving and connecting with people.
  • Tell us how we can be supportive of your efforts.
Watch for updates in July and August. Also, with your consent, your nonprofit will be showcased so that Great Hearts community members start to get to know about your organization.
Thanks for all the good work that you are doing.

About Us

At Great Hearts Initiatives™, we believe that everyone has something to give. We also believe that the desire to give is natural and fundamental to the human spirit. By bringing together those who are helping others, and supporting their efforts, Great Hearts Initiatives hopes to expand their abilities to make lasting impacts and live healthy, fulfilling lives.
Our story is really the collective stories of many people coming together to make a difference. The compassion and dedication shown by greathearted people is our inspiration. As an innovator in ways to engage people, and as a performance accelerator for nonprofits and communities, Great Hearts Initiatives™ helps those who are doing good to do more, better.

Our Philosophy

We believe that the desire to give is natural and fundamental to the human spirit.
The compassion and dedication shown by greathearted people is our inspiration.
As an innovator in ways to engage people, and as an accelerator for nonprofit organizations, GHI helps those doing good to do more, better.
GHI expands individual, organizational and community capabilities by:
  • Getting more people involved in a giving and its health benefits.
  • Building a better understanding of the many ways to give beyond financially.
  • Amplifying the important work of causes, nonprofit organizations and social enterprises regardless of their size.
  • Creating giving systems that maximize the amount of the contribution going to the cause or charity and reducing overhead expenses.
  • Providing strategic and operational services to nonprofits and social enterprises.
GHI prefers to partner with other organizations whenever possible. By building upon and amplifying existing efforts, GHI hopes to enhance the ecosystem of giving.

Our Business Model

GHI is a social enterprise—a for profit business that address social issues. GHI believes that the social enterprise model is the right fit for our mission and goals. GHI is privately held and encourages investment in nonprofits and community-based organizations rather than in us.

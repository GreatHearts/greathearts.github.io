Great Hearts Community Beta Launch

The Great Hearts Community Beta Launch is underway

Thanks for being part of the beta test! We appreciate your help in developing the community.
A beta test or launch refers to computer software that is undergoing testing and has not yet been officially released. Beta software is only available to a select group of people. Since the Great Hearts community software is a pre-release version of the final application, you may experience some glitches or “bugs”. If so, let us know.
Please send any issues, feedback, and ideas to beta@greathearts.community.
